ABOUT THE HACKATHON

A focused, problem-solving hackathon that brings together curious minds to build meaningful tech solutions under real-world constraints. Organized by the Marvel Cart Club at JECRC Foundation, the hackathon encourages participants to explore emerging technologies, collaborate across domains, and turn ideas into working prototypes. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced developer, the event is designed to challenge your thinking, sharpen your skills, and provide a platform to learn, build, and compete in a high-energy environment.

WHO CAN PARTICIPATE?

Eligibility

Open to everyone. Students, professionals, and independent developers from any background are welcome to participate.

Age Requirement

All participants must be 18 years or older at the time of registration.

Team Composition

Teams of up to 3 members are allowed. Participants may join solo or form a team.

FAQs

Who can participate in the AMD Slingshot?
+

Clash of Coders is open to students, professionals, and independent developers from any college, university, organization, or background. There are no restrictions on specialization or prior experience. Anyone with an interest in building innovative tech solutions is welcome to participate.

Do I need to have a team to register?
+

No, having a team is not mandatory. Participants can register individually or as part of a team. Teams can have 1 to 3 members, and solo participants are equally encouraged to participate.

Is there a registration fee?
+

Yes, a nominal registration fee of INR 300/- per team is applicable to confirm participation in the hackathon. Only teams that complete the payment process will be considered for evaluation and shortlisting.

How will the shortlisted teams be informed?
+

Shortlisted teams will be informed via registered email and official communication channels.

Where will the Grand Finale take place?
+

The Grand Finale will be held at JECRC Foundation, Jaipur.
